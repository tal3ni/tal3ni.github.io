Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Phone number (for authentication)
  • Name
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Vehicle license plate numbers
  • Vehicle details (make, model, color — optional)

Automatically Collected

  • Device information (type, OS version)
  • App usage data (searches performed, features used)
  • Search history (license plates you have searched, and the time of each search)
  • Log data (IP address, access times)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account via SMS
  • Display your contact information when someone searches for your vehicle
  • Improve the App and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

Public Information

When someone searches for a vehicle you have registered:

  • Your name (if provided) is visible
  • Your phone number is visible
  • Your profile photo (if uploaded) is visible
  • Your vehicle details (if provided) are visible

We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your phone number for marketing (unless you opt-in)

We MAY Share With

  • Service providers (hosting, SMS delivery)
  • Law enforcement (if legally required)
  • Business partners (with your explicit consent)

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication (OTP via SMS)
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular security audits

However, no method is 100% secure. Use the service at your own risk.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights: Settings → Profile → Edit Profile, or contact noreply.tal3ni@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal requirements: some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Children's Privacy

Tal3ni is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, and file storage)
  • Twilio (SMS delivery)

These services have their own privacy policies.

9. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Egypt, including the United States.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

Questions about privacy?

  • Email: noreply.tal3ni@gmail.com
  • In-app: Settings → Help & Support
